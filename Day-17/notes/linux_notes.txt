# Linux Notes - Day 17

Command: history

Purpose:
Shows previously executed commands.

Examples:

history
Shows all command history.

history 10
Shows the last 10 commands.

!!

Runs the previous command again.

!25

Runs command number 25 from history.

Useful for:
- Reusing old commands.
- Saving typing time.
- Finding forgotten commands.
- Debugging mistakes.
